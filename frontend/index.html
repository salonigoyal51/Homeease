Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services.

What is HomeEase Service Management System? expand_more

HomeEase is a digital platform used to list, manage, and book local services like electricians and plumbers. It helps users find verified professionals easily and allows providers to manage bookings efficiently.

What details are required to book a service? expand_more

Users need to provide: • Service Type • Location & Address • Preferred Date & Time • Specific requirements/issues. These details help professionals understand the job before arriving.

Can users schedule visits or site inspections? expand_more

Yes, users can schedule service visits by selecting a preferred date and time. This ensures that the professional arrives when you are available, avoiding any confusion.

Is the pricing transparent? expand_more

Yes, we believe in transparency. You will see the estimated cost before booking, and final charges are based on the work performed, ensuring no hidden costs.
